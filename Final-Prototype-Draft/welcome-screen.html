Post-Disaster Support

This program aims to link trained volunteers with people who are looking for support with their mental health after going through a disaster or another tough event. Our program is set up to offer short one-on-one talks with volunteers who have experience in mental health care. The goal is to guide you to other helpful resources based on what you need and prefer.
